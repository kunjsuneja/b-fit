Glutes Workout

Squats

Squats are a compound exercise that targets the glutes, quadriceps, and hamstrings. Perform 3 sets of 10-12 reps.

Lunges

Lunges are effective for toning and strengthening the glutes. Perform 3 sets of 10-12 reps on each leg.

Glute Bridge

Glute bridge isolates and strengthens the glutes. Perform 3 sets of 12-15 reps.

Donkey Kicks

Donkey kicks target the glutes and can help improve muscle definition. Perform 3 sets of 12-15 reps on each leg.

Fire Hydrants

Fire hydrants engage the glutes and outer thighs. Perform 3 sets of 12-15 reps on each leg.
