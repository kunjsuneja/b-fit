Triceps Workout

Tricep Dips

Tricep dips target the triceps muscles. Perform 3 sets of 10-12 reps.

Tricep Kickbacks

Tricep kickbacks isolate the triceps. Perform 3 sets of 10-12 reps.

Close-Grip Push-Ups

Close-grip push-ups primarily work the triceps. Perform 3 sets of 10-12 reps.

Overhead Tricep Extension

Overhead tricep extension targets the triceps. Perform 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
