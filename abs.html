Crunches

Crunches target the rectus abdominis (the 'six-pack' muscles) and also engage the obliques. Perform 3 sets of 15 reps. Watch how to do Crunches

Leg Raises

Leg raises mainly work the lower abdominal muscles. Do 3 sets of 12 reps. Watch how to do Leg Raises

Plank

The plank is an isometric exercise that strengthens the core muscles. Hold the position for 30 seconds, repeat 3 times. Watch how to do Plank

Russian Twists

Russian twists target the obliques. Perform 3 sets of 15 twists on each side. Watch how to do Russian Twists

Bicycle Crunches

Bicycle crunches engage the entire core area. Do 3 sets of 20 reps (10 reps per side). Watch how to do Bicycle Crunches

Reverse Crunches

Reverse crunches target the lower abs. Perform 3 sets of 12 reps. Watch how to do Reverse Crunches
