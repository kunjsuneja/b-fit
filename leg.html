Squats

Squats are a compound exercise that primarily target the muscles in your thighs, hips, and buttocks.

Lunges

Lunges are great for strengthening your quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. They also help improve balance.

Deadlifts

Deadlifts primarily work your hamstrings, lower back, and glutes. They also engage your core muscles for stability.

Calf Raises

Calf raises target the calf muscles and help strengthen and define your lower legs.

Leg Press

Leg press is an effective exercise for targeting your quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes.

Step Ups

Step ups help strengthen your leg muscles and improve your balance and stability.

Leg Curl

Leg curls target the hamstrings and are essential for balanced leg strength.

Box Jumps

Box jumps are a plyometric exercise that helps improve explosive power in your legs.
